Quick Overview of Stronger Types

Even though all of the pokemon have their own strengths and weaknesses there are of course some that are stronger than others. Generally speaking, Dragon is typically one of the strongest types in pokemon. This is because of its great defensive abilities. Another type that is very defensively strong is Steel. Thanks to this pokemon such as Dialga is one of the stronger pokemon in terms of defensive abilities. This is because it is a dragon/steel type combination. Here is a picture of dialga.
